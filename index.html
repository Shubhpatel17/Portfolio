About Me

Designing with purpose, building with precision.

I'm a product designer and full-stack developer with over 7 years of experience creating end-to-end digital experiences for startups and Fortune 500 companies alike. I believe great products live at the intersection of thoughtful design and clean engineering.

My approach is grounded in user research, validated through rapid prototyping, and delivered through scalable code. I've led design systems, shipped mobile apps to millions of users, and built the teams that made it all possible.

When I'm not at my desk, I'm writing about design systems, mentoring junior designers, or hiking the trails around the Bay Area.
